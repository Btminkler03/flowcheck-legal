FlowCheck Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026

FlowCheck stores all financial data locally on your device using device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial information on any server we control.

AI Advisor

When you use Nova AI Advisor, a financial snapshot is sent to Anthropic, PBC to generate responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

Delete all data inside the app: Settings → Delete All Data. Deleting the app also removes all data.
